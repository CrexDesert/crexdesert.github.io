Щоб скористатися послугами евакуатора, водій транспортного засобу повинен мати на руках паспорт (або інший документ, що підтверджує особу), водійські права, свідоцтво реєстрації автівки, доручення на її керування, (якщо не є власником). Preparation for the arrival of the tow truck

After ordering a tow truck, it's important to prepare the vehicle for transportation. Remove money, valuables, and, if possible, cargo from the trunk.

To use tow truck services, prepare the necessary documents: driver's license, vehicle registration certificate, and a power of attorney for driving if you are not the owner of the vehicle. Without these documents, the towing service will not be able to provide the service.

While waiting for the arrival of the special equipment, stay close to your vehicle. After the specialists arrive, fill out the transportation power of attorney form. If there are any damages, describe them. After that, transportation will take place, and it is not necessary to accompany the vehicle during evacuation.

What documents are required to call a tow truck?

To use tow truck services, the vehicle driver must have their passport (or other identification), driver's license, vehicle registration certificate, and a power of attorney for driving the vehicle (if not the owner). If the customer cannot accompany the vehicle, they must provide a transportation power of attorney and hand over the documents and car keys to the driver.

How to call a tow truck?

You can order tow truck services in Vinnytsia by phone or online through our website. The operator will quickly accept the order and select the appropriate tow truck for your vehicle. Alternatively, you can fill out a special form on the company’s website online.

Can passengers ride in the tow truck?
